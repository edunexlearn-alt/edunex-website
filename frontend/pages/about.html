Our Story

Who We Are

Edunex Academy was founded in 2015 with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between traditional schooling and real-world skill development. What started as a small coaching centre has now grown into a full-fledged educational institute serving hundreds of students across India.

We offer a unique blend of academic coaching (Class 5–12) and professional computer science courses. Our approach is student-first — we believe every child learns differently, and our curriculum is designed to accommodate that diversity.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, high-quality education that empowers students to excel academically and develop the skills needed for a rapidly evolving world—from foundational school subjects to cutting-edge computer science.
